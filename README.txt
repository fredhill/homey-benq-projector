Bring your BenQ network projector into Homey and automate it like any other smart device. Power it on and off, switch inputs, adjust volume, toggle ECO Blank, change picture modes, and track lamp hours with Insights — all over your local network, no extra hardware required.

Compatible with BenQ network projectors from approximately 2012–2019 that have LAN Control enabled in their settings. Confirmed working on the BenQ SH915. Auto-discovers your projector on the local network during setup, with a manual IP option as fallback.
